Composed 10th century in northern France; manuscript copied 10th century in Strasbourg, Alsace. Source manuscript: Augsburg, Stadtarchiv, Urkundensammlung no. 5.
Passion poem, Augsburg manuscript (PassAugsb). In T. Rainsford (ed.) Old Gallo-Romance Corpus, version 1.0. Stuttgart, Institut für Linguistik/Romanistik, 2025. http://ogr-corpus.org. 2025. http://ogr-corpus.org.

The transcription and analysis is based on Berschin et al. (1981). The text is written in very small, faded characters in a single line. It was present on the parchment before a charter was copied in 1067.
